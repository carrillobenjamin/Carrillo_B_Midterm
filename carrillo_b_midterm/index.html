iPhone vs Android



Why iPhone is Amazing!

Firstly, iphones and all Apple products intergrate seamlessly with icloud. icloud makes it super easy to send files,
images etc. from iPhone to iPhone, or any Apple product.


Another reason why Apple is better is because of the clean, sleek design of ios and macOS. Android has a less
modern and expensive feeling through the design.





Why iPhone Sucks!

THE CAMERA! Android Galaxy has always had a better camera for as long as I can remember. Not only is the
camera better but you can also change your camera settings on the spot unlike Apple.


Who cares about the ios design anyways! On Android you can chnage the look of everything on your
phone: font, text size, app designs and so much more.
